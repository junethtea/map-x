about=Map-X (Lite Version) is a powerful spatial comparison tool tailored for Telecommunications, RF Engineering, and Network Optimization professionals. It transforms the standard QGIS interface into a dynamic command center, allowing users to compare datasets side-by-side without the hassle of toggling layers. Key features include:
    * Dual View Engine: Compare two maps side-by-side with draggable splitters. (Triple and Quad views are available in the Pro version).
    * Bi-Directional Live Sync: Panning and zooming in one map instantly synchronizes across the active canvas, ensuring you never lose spatial context.
    * Intelligent Statistical Legend: An auto-generating, compact legend that calculates real-time data distribution (Sample Count & Percentage) based strictly on the active canvas extent and priority layers.
    * Smart Layering (Z-Order): Automatically forces critical physical infrastructure data (Sites, Gcells) to the top layer, ensuring they are never obscured by drive test data or coverage polygons.
    * Ultra-Minimalist UI: Designed to maximize map visibility. (Layout Export and Crosshair Tracking features are available in the Pro version).
    Upgrade to the Pro Version via Gumroad or Lynk.id to unlock up to 4 synchronized views, crosshair tracking, and layout exporting!
